Josh Neale is a kit player, percussionist and graduate of the London College of Music, having specialised in jazz.

With origins in brass bands, he has been fortunate enough to have toured Europe multiple times, and to swiftly progress into playing as part of a Big Band, notably Romsey Area Youth Jazz Orchestra who performed in Cork during it's 'Red Hot International Jazz Festival' in 2012.

Though experienced in studio and live recording, performing is his main passion, whether that’s on the main stage of Ealing Jazz Festival, or in a theatre pit. Now with recording credits such as The Skelton & Skinner Septet’s The Odd Couple, and several shows under his belt, such as West Side Story, Will Todd’s Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland, Half a Sixpence, Oliver, Rags and Bat Boy, he hopes to continue this wide array of musical projects alongside teaching at the Junior London College of Music and in association with Ealing Music Service.
