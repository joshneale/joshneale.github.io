A bit about me

I have been playing drums since I was 10 years old (I'm 18 as of 2014). Originally I wanted to play guitar so my Dad took me to a 'music school open day' where I could try out guitar, drums and bass. I took to drums straight away. I got my first drum kit a year later (and still have it to this day) and that's about the time I joined the Hamble Area Youth Band, my local brass band.

From playing in HAYB and various school bands I went on to join Hampshire County Youth Brass Band and Romsey Area Youth Jazz Ensemble. I learnt a great deal and gained a lot of experience rehearsing, performing and touring with these bands and am therefore so grateful for the opputunity.
